About Us

We developed a GPA calculator exclusively for South Forsyth High School students, this GPA calculator is a collaborative creation by Suhas Devarapalli, Aarin Karamchandani, and Pranav Maturi. Our interface makes it simple for students to accurately compute their GPA for each high school year, from 9th through 12th grade, providing both weighted and unweighted outcomes. It's a tool designed to help South Forsyth students manage and track their academic performance with ease.
